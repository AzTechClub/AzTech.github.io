Zen.py

Domain: 9-12
Team(s) Per School: 1
Participant(s) Allowed Per Team: 4
Mode: Online + Offline
Stages: 2

Stage 1
The contest will be hosted on Hackerrank. The contest will comprise of easy/medium level questions.
Each question has points allocated to it, get all the test cases right to score maximum points.
Top 8 teams will qualify for the final round.
The whole team will participate from a single hackerrank account.
If in case 2 teams have the same score then the leaderboard will be adjusted according to the aggregate time taken by them.

Stage 2
The final round will be a project based round.
The project will be algorithmic or an AI based project.
The participants will be provided with a prompt on the day of the prelims or the next day.
TThey will have to make a project and present it at the venue on the day of the final.
The teams will be given ample of time to complete the projects.
Adding a GUI might help in case of a tie.


NOTE
Participants can be asked to explain their code at any point of time
In case the participants are caught copying from the internet or cheating by any means, it would lead to immediate disqualification of the team .


Judgement Criteria
Authenticity
Creativity
Efficiency of the code (shorter/clean code will fetch more points)

InnoVate

Domain:9-12
Team(s) Per School: 1
Participant(s) Allowed Per Team: 5
Mode: Online + Offline
Stages: 1

Guidelines
Participants will have to derive a sustainable business plan based on the description provided.

Participants will have to complete the following tasks:

Task 1: Make a promotional video of the business(60-90 seconds). The Format should be .mp4/.wmv. All the source clips must be provided as well
Task 2: Make a prototype of the application or website along with the UI/UX file.
Task 3: Advertisement Banners
Task 4: Team Apparels

Tasks 3 and 4 are optional but they do carry extra points.

On the day of the event, the participants will have 3-4 minutes to pitch their business plan to the judges.

The pitching can be assisted with a powerpoint presentation.

Pitching will be followed by a QnA round.

About AzTech

AzTech was an initiative taken by 4 students. Formed in the summer of 2021, it is the Tech Community of K.R. Mangalam World School, Vikaspuri.
