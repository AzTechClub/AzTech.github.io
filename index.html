ABOUT US


VELOCITY is the annual techfest organised by AzTech Club of K.R. Mangalam World School, Vikaspuri. It is a modern opportunity for Tech fanatics to showcase their skills and have a fair competitive enviornment with other participants. We have the fortunate opportunity to host you all for this event. We eagerly await your participation and hope that the event is a success and every participant is given a fair shot at the chance of victory and we sincerely counsel all the participants to engage in the event fairly and have fun.

About AzTech

AzTech was an initiative taken by 4 students. Formed in the summer of 2021, it is the Tech Community of K.R. Mangalam World School, Vikaspuri. The club which is a safe and knowledgable environment helps the enthusiasts explore their interests in Information Technology. The club provides enthusiasts with a wide variety of opportunities to showcase their talents in various technological fields.
